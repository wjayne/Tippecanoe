About this tool



The Tippecanoe County Assessor’s Office Income Approach Report Tool is designed to display the information used to derive an assessed value via the income approach for the most recent assessment date. The majority of commercial and industrial properties in Tippecanoe County are valued on the income approach, which is a valuation method that predicts how much an investor would be willing to pay a piece of real-estate based on the income and risks associated with ownership.

Mass appraisal property valuation in Indiana legislatively requires that we acquire information from multiple sources regarding the various factors that may affect a property’s valuation. The normalized aggregate data derived is to be applied equitably across similar property types to ensure a fair and balanced approach to assessed valuation for taxing purposes. Toward that end, if you are a commercial property owner willing to aid in our collection efforts please visit tippecanoeassessorsurvey.com

Ultimately, our office believes that working in a collaborative effort with local experts to source data on our market will ensure transparent and equitable assessed values for commercial/industrial properties. Having an open dialog with experts in the field is essential for our office to stay up to date with trends that affect property value, and we hope that relying on local industry leaders will help us to keep tax-payers more informed about exactly how their assessed valuations are reached. If you have any questions regarding the reporting tool or commercial/industrial property valuation, please call our office at (765)423-9255 or email our office at assesstool@tippecanoe.in.gov
